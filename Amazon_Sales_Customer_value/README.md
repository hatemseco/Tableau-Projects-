# Amazon Sales & Customer Value Analysis

## Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes Amazon sales data with a focus on customer value and purchasing behavior. The objective is to identify high-value customers, revenue drivers, and patterns that support strategic sales and marketing decisions.

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Customer Segments
- Revenue Contribution by Customer
- Sales Trends Over Time

## Key Insights
- A small segment of customers contributes a disproportionately large share of total revenue, highlighting the importance of high-value customer retention.
- Sales performance varies significantly across customer segments, indicating opportunities for targeted marketing strategies.
- Revenue trends show consistent growth with identifiable peak periods, supporting demand forecasting and planning.
- Customer value analysis reveals opportunities to optimize pricing and promotional efforts.
