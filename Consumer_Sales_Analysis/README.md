# Consumer Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of consumer sales performance across cities, product categories, and time. The objective is to understand sales distribution, identify top-performing regions and products, and uncover seasonal sales patterns.

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Profit
- Sales by City
- Sales by Category
- Monthly Sales Trends

## Key Insights
- Sales performance varies significantly across cities, with a small number of cities contributing the majority of total revenue.
- Certain product categories consistently outperform others, highlighting key drivers of consumer demand.
- Sales exhibit strong seasonal patterns, with peak performance occurring toward the end of the year.
- Profit trends generally align with revenue trends, indicating healthy overall sales performance.
