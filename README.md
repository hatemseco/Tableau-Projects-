# Tableau Projects Portfolio

## Overview
This folder contains a collection of Tableau dashboards focused on exploratory data analysis, business intelligence, and data-driven storytelling. Each project addresses a real-world business problem and demonstrates the ability to transform data into clear, actionable insights through effective visualization.

The projects cover sales performance, customer behavior, operational monitoring, churn analysis, and service optimization.

---

## Projects Included

### 1. Amazon Sales & Customer Value Analysis
Analyzes customer segmentation and revenue contribution to identify high-value customers and long-term growth opportunities.

**Key Focus:** Customer segmentation, revenue trends, lifetime value analysis

---

### 2. Consumer Sales Analysis
Provides a comprehensive view of consumer sales performance across cities, product categories, and seasonal trends.

**Key Focus:** Sales analytics, regional performance, category insights

---

### 3. Smart Manufacturing Operations Dashboard
Monitors production, quality, and network performance metrics to support operational efficiency and real-time decision-making.

**Key Focus:** Operational analytics, quality monitoring, performance tracking

---

### 4. Telecom Customer Churn & Retention Analysis
Examines customer churn patterns using tenure, pricing, and satisfaction metrics to identify key retention drivers.

**Key Focus:** Churn analysis, customer retention, satisfaction metrics

---

### 5. Tips Data Analysis Dashboard
Explores tipping behavior in a restaurant setting based on time, day, party size, and customer demographics.

**Key Focus:** Behavioral analysis, service optimization, revenue patterns
