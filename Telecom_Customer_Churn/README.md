# Telecom Customer Churn & Retention Analysis

## Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes customer churn patterns in the telecom industry using tenure, pricing, and satisfaction metrics. The objective is to identify key drivers of churn and provide insights to improve customer retention strategies.

## Key KPIs
- Total Customers
- Churn Rate (%)
- Average Monthly Charges
- Customer Tenure
- Average Satisfaction Score (Churned Customers)

## Key Insights
- Customer churn is highest during the early months of service, indicating onboarding as a critical risk period.
- As customer tenure increases, churn rates stabilize, suggesting stronger loyalty among long-term customers.
- Churned customers exhibit lower satisfaction scores, highlighting dissatisfaction as a major churn driver.
- Early-stage retention initiatives could significantly reduce overall churn and improve customer lifetime value.
