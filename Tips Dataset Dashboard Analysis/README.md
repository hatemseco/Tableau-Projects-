# Tips Data Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes tipping behavior in a restaurant setting based on time, day, party size, and customer demographics. The objective is to understand factors that influence tipping patterns and support operational decisions related to staffing and service optimization.

## Key KPIs
- Total Tips
- Total Bill Amount
- Tips by Day of Week
- Tips by Time (Lunch vs Dinner)
- Tips by Party Size
- Revenue by Customer Demographics

## Key Insights
- Dinner service generates significantly higher total revenue and tips compared to lunch service.
- Weekend days are associated with higher tipping amounts, indicating peak service periods.
- Larger party sizes contribute higher total tips, supporting staffing adjustments during busy times.
- Tipping behavior varies across customer demographics, providing insights for targeted service improvements.
